## Important Notes for AI

1. **Tondi vs Jiwa**: Always use "tondi" in Indonesian context, never "jiwa" (generic Indonesian word)
2. **Ulos**: Never translate - always keep as "ulos" in all languages
3. **Dalihan Na Tolu**: Never translate - this is the sacred kinship system
4. **Horas**: Universal greeting, same in all languages
5. **Andaliman**: The unique Batak pepper - never substitute with other pepper names
